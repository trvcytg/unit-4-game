Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a randomly chosen number, you lose if your total score goes above the randomly chosen number.

The value of each crystal is hidden, pay attention to how your score reacts to each crystal!

Any time a game begins, the value of each crystal will be reassigned to a new random value!
